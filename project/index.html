About Game

Dota2 is a popular multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game developed and published by Valve Corporation. The game has become one of the most popular and recognized projects in the world of computer games, attracting millions of players from all over the world. Dota 2 is based on a popular custom map for Warcraft III called Defense of the Ancients (DotA). The game involves two teams, each consisting of five players. The goal of the game is to destroy the enemy's ancient temple, called Ancient, while protecting your own.
