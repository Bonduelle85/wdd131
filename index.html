Perm, Russian Federation

The city of Perm recently celebrated its 300th anniversary. Here are some facts about this city. The word “Perm”, according to one version, comes from the word “parma” - an elevated area overgrown with spruce. “Permyak salty ears” is a traditional nickname for the inhabitants of the Perm lands. The geological term "Permian" was named after this city. In Perm there is the world's largest cast iron cannon - the Perm "Tsar Cannon", cast in 1869 for one of the frigates. Perm has the same street layout as New York - a “grid”.
